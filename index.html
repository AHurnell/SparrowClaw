Why build this business?

The proliferation of low-cost commercial drones as weapons of war has changed the battlefield. Fibre-optic and AI drones — immune to jamming — are now central to offensive operations, yet most armed forces lack affordable, scalable tools to counter them.

Sparrow Laser addresses this gap directly. By developing compact, low-cost laser and light dazzler systems, we give front-line units a practical defence that can be fielded at scale — turning an acute vulnerability into a manageable threat.
